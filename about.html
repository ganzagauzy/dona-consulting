    About Us

    We are consulting firm incorporated under the laws of Republic of Rwanda and having its principal place of business at Kigali City, Gasabo District.
    We serve private businesses, public institutions; as far as non-governmental organizations.
    We help clients in diverse industries to improve their performance, transform their enterprises and reduce costs, leverage technology, process and review large amounts of complex data, address regulatory changes, recover from distress and stimulate growth. We help our clients to improve performance, comply with complex regulations and reduce costs delivering measurable and sustainable results.

    We have a diverse team of highly qualified and specialized professionals and consultants. The team has technical knowledge and understanding.

    We provide and develops both standard and customized solutions together with value adding ideas intended to enable our clients achieve their objectives
